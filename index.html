What we do

Specialists in the technologies that change how people learn and work

Virtual & Mixed Reality

Immersive training, product visualisation and simulation. We have delivered world-first VR experiences — including remote, hardware-based clinical simulation used by universities worldwide.

Haptics

Touch is the frontier of realism. We advise on and build haptic-enabled training systems that let learners feel and practise skilled, physical procedures — safely and repeatedly.

Robotics

MBE

The founder

James Markey MBE

Founder & Principal Consultant

James founded JP Mentors in November 2017 to bring world-class training and technology consultancy to organisations of every size. An award-winning technologist and entrepreneur, he has pioneered the use of virtual reality and haptics in professional training — including the world's first portable haptic and VR dental simulator, now used to train clinicians across dozens of universities.

Recognised with an MBE for his contribution to technology and training, James blends the discipline of classic mentorship with a restless curiosity for what comes next in VR, haptics, robotics and AI.
